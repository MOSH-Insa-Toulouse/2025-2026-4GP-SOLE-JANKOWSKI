### Introduction  
This project has the aim of developing a low-tech graphite-based tactile sensor. The research conducted by Cheng-Wei Lin, Zhibo Zhao, Jeamyung Kim and Jixianf Huang states that graphite pencil traces drawn on print papers have the opportunity to act as strain gauges. 

As the strain is applied, the graphite structure changes, which in turn modifies the electrical resistance in the material. 


It is part of the formation at the Physics Department at Institut National des Sciences Appliquées in Toulouse.
